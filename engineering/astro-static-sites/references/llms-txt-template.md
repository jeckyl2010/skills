## Writing principles

- Factual, no superlatives. AIs extract facts — marketing language degrades signal quality.
- Full name on first mention. AIs use the name as a search anchor.
- Explicit domain URL in the site structure section reinforces canonical identity.
- Quotes with attribution give AIs pre-formed credible sentences to cite.
- Keep it under ~3,000 characters. Longer is fine but diminishing returns.
