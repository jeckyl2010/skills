# Rovo Chat Macro — Beta Status (May 2025)

Source: https://community.atlassian.com/forums/Confluence-questions/Rovo-Chat-macro-Confluence-Cloud/qaq-p/2916987

## Thread timeline

**Apr 3 2025 — Gareth B (user)**
"I just saw a demo of Confluence where there's an option to add an AI chat window to a page using the macro /rovochat or /rovo chat. I believe this scopes the chat to the particular space the page is in."

**Apr 3 2025 — Liam C (Atlassian Team Member)**
"We're trying to determine the right experience. The concept is that you can embed a Chat experience on a page which allows visitors to ask questions. It would be scoped to things like a particular space, label, or ancestor page. We're looking at releasing this in beta in Q2 2025."

**May 14 2025 — Matt B (Atlassian Team Member)**
"The Rovo Chat macro for Confluence is currently in beta and available to a limited number of customers. We're actively working on the feature and expect to gradually expand access. I'll post more details here once we have more to share."

## Summary
- Macro invoked via `/rovochat` or `/rovo chat` in the Confluence editor
- Scoping options: space, label, ancestor page
- Status as of May 2025: limited private beta, gradual rollout, no GA date confirmed
- To test availability: type `/rovo` in the editor on your Confluence instance

## Related
- Rovo Chat sidebar panel: GA, available on all pages, accessible from right sidebar
- Atlassian Intelligence: requires Premium/Enterprise, site-admin toggle
